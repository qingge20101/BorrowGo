Help & FAQ

What if I accidentally damage an item?

You can choose Damage Protection at checkout. If damage happens, we’ll guide you through a simple claim process.

Do I need to clean the item before returning?

Light cleaning is appreciated, but we handle professional cleaning and maintenance after each rental.

How does deposit escrow work?

Deposits are held securely and automatically released after the return inspection is completed.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes. Cancellation rules vary by item and date. In this demo, cancellation is always allowed before pickup day.
